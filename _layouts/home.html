Research Software Engineers (RSEs) are the people behind research software. This website is designed as a hub for Sheffield academics seeking help with research software and as a community for research software engineers.

Slides for the above presentation

How can Sheffield RSEs help?

We can help maximise your research impact through good research software. Consultancy, development, training, grant support, and technical support are just a few ways we can help.

See the activities page for more details about our services. You can also book a code clinic, subscribe to our mailing list or follow us on social media below.
