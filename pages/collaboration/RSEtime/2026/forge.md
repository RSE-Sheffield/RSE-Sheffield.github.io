Focused Optimisation and Research software Guidance Engagement

### Introduction

The RSE FORGE (Focused Optimisation and Research software Guidance Engagement) projects are free, short duration collaborations between research active staff within the University of Sheffield and Research Software Engineers (RSEs) working in the RSE team. 

This program aims to support researchers within the University of Sheffield with software that would benefit from short focused bursts of RSE time to help refine, optimise, or package their research software.

### Eligibility Criteria

- Be a research active member of staff affiliated to the University of Sheffield.
- Applicants must have accessible research code that works as intended.
